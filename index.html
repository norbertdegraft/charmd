Who We Serve?

We provide services for African immigrants and other people of color. With our support, they will be provided with a safe and welcoming environment where they will be guided in addressing their and mental health issues and improve the quality of their lives. Immigrant youth, families, adults, and couples including people of color are our targeted group to enable them cope with emotional, behavioral, and interpersonal challenges by employing evidence- based practices that are shown by research to result in positive long-term outcomes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality behavioral health care services to a diverse community of immigrants and people of color. A welcoming and pleasant environment, filled with an aroma of all-inclusiveness, Care, Hope, Act of love, Respect, Massive Dedication (Charmd).

Our Practice

Charmd Behavioral Healthcare was created with the goal of helping African and other immigrants as well as other people of color who do not have the interest for the traditional set up to receive quality behavioral health services. As a practice, we adopt a supportive approach. Meaning, each of our clients is our partner in addressing their unique needs. We believe that everyone is unique; therefore, we meet each client halfway to create comfortability and a welcoming environment to satisfy their need.

Therapy Session

Dialectical Behavior Therapy

DBT is a type of cognitive therapy that focus on increasing your ability to stay in the present moment, improving your effectiveness in managing stress, building healthy relationships through improved communication, and increasing positive emotional experiences.

Prolonged Exposure

PE is a type of exposure therapy that treats Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder. It uses techniques to help the person manage situations, thoughts and feelings that remind them of the trauma and relearn that most situations are safe.

Cognitive Behavior Therapy

CBT is a goal-oriented process which teaches you to identify negative thinking patterns. Thoughts and feelings play a fundamental role in your behavior, CBT uses strategies to promote and sustain your ability for objective, realistic thinking, which improves your happiness in life.

Acceptance and Commitment Therapy

ACT teaches you the mindfulness skills to help you live and behave in ways that are consistent with your own personal values while developing psychological flexibility.

Motivational Interviewing

MI has the goal of increasing your desire for change. Your therapist actively listens nonjudgmentally and follows your lead as you explore your values and motivation for making changes in your life.

ERP (EX/RP)

ERP is a type of exposure therapy that is evidence-based in treating Obsessive- Compulsive Disorder. It uses techniques to help a person gradually experience triggers to obsessive, unwanted thoughts while not engaging in compulsions that temporarily reduce anxiety but make OCD more distressing in the long run.
